Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Summary: HAOCHI is designed to keep access credentials on your device. We do not require account registration and we do not sell personal information.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the HAOCHI mobile application ("the App") handles information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

HAOCHI does not ask you to create an online account. The App may store the following locally on your device:

  • Access credentials you choose during setup (for example, a library access code and a quick-browse PIN).
  • App preferences and optional saved items you create while using the App.

We do not intentionally collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment details through the App.

3. Network and Browsing Activity

When you browse websites inside the App, network requests are made directly between your device and the websites you visit, similar to a standard web browser. The App may perform network checks related to security features you enable.

We do not operate a centralized service that stores your full browsing history on our servers.

4. How Information Is Used

Information stored on your device is used only to provide App functionality, such as:

  • Unlocking secure and quick-browse modes.
  • Performing optional network or VPN verification before protected browsing.
  • Saving bookmarks, notes, or similar content you choose to keep in the App.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Standard iOS system services (for example, network connectivity) may process technical data required for the App to function.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored locally remains on your device until you delete it, reset your keys inside the App, or uninstall the App. Uninstalling the App removes locally stored App data from the device in the ordinary course of iOS operation.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to help protect information stored on the device. No method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or HAOCHI, contact us at info@520apps.com.
